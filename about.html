About Our Project

Kisaan Sahayak is an innovative platform aimed at assisting farmers in making informed decisions for better farming outcomes. Our system leverages AI and data analysis to recommend crops based on weather conditions, soil type, and local mineral composition in the land. We also provide farmers with personalized information about government schemes available in their state, district, and country to maximize benefits.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology and knowledge, reducing guesswork in agriculture and increasing productivity while promoting sustainable farming practices.

About Our Team

We are a group of 6 dedicated students from Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, Madhya Pradesh. Together, we are passionate about using technology to make a positive impact in agriculture and empower the farming community with AI-driven solutions.

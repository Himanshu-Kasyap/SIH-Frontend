Crop Recommendations

Get the best crops for your farm based on soil type and weather conditions:

  • Sandy Soil: Groundnut, Potato, Carrot
  • Clay Soil: Paddy, Wheat, Mustard
  • Loamy Soil: Cotton, Sugarcane, Tomato
  • Current Weather: Rainy season crops include Paddy, Maize, and Pulses

Government Schemes

  • PM-KISAN: ₹6000 per year for small & marginal farmers
  • PMFBY: Crop insurance with low premium rates
  • Kisan Credit Card (KCC): Easy loans for farming needs
  • Soil Health Card Scheme: Know your soil’s nutrient status

Loan & Financial Support

Farmers can avail loans at the lowest interest rates from:

  • State Bank of India (SBI): Kisan Credit Card at ~4% interest
  • Punjab National Bank: Crop loans with government subsidy
  • Co-operative Banks: Short-term seasonal crop loans
